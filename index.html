What is Waste to Electricity?

Waste to electricity is a way to turn trash into power! Instead of letting waste go to landfills, we can use it to create energy.

There are different ways to do this, like burning waste to create heat or using biogas from food scraps.

How it Works

Here's a simplified explanation:

  1. Waste is collected and sorted.
  2. The waste is processed to make it ready for energy conversion.
  3. The waste is converted into energy, like heat or biogas.
  4. This energy is used to generate electricity.

Why it's Important

Waste to electricity helps us:

  • Reduce the amount of trash in landfills.
  • Create clean energy.
  • Help the environment.
